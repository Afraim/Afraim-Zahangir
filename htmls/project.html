Water Adventure of Tory the Turtle

Tory likes to collect worms but is afraid of sea urchins and jellyfishes. Help Tory collect all 40 worms.


"Water Adventure of Tory the Turtle" is a construct3 game (https://www.construct.net/en) for PC users only. It is a simple project that resembles a maze game where you play as Tory the turtle and move around the water to collect worms but be careful of the urchins and jellyfishes.

Controls are basic: When the game starts press enter to play. In the rules layout press any key to continue. Use the arrow keys to control.
